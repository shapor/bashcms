Community/Developer Information

Tux3 is a grassroots open source project which has a diverse community with the common goal of seeking to do storage on Linux better - much better. We are strong believers that an open community is just as important as open source code. If you want to join the community, or just learn more about Tux3, log on to our IRC channel and mailing list below.

Communication

  • irc://irc.oftc.net/#tux3 - #tux3 IRC channel on oftc.net logs. Tux3-U is held here Tuesday and Thursday nights at 20:00 Pacific time.
